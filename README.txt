GUY GAUVREAU — MEMORIAL SLIDESHOW

To start:
1. Double-click “Start Memorial Slideshow.command”.
2. If macOS asks for confirmation, choose Open.
3. In the browser, click “Start slideshow”.

The Start button enters full screen, keeps the display awake, and enables audio
for videos. Move the mouse to reveal the controls.

Keyboard controls:
Space       Pause or resume
Left/Right  Previous or next
M           Mute or unmute video audio
F           Full screen
Escape      Leave full screen

The slideshow contains 319 photos and 2 videos. Photos display for five seconds
with a gentle crossfade. Videos play from beginning to end with their audio.
The complete sequence loops continuously.

Files with parentheses, square brackets, or braces in their names are excluded.
The original Dropbox folder is not changed.
